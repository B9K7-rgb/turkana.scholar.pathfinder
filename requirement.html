üèõ University Admission Requirements

Public Universities

C+ Minimum for Degree Programs
C Minimum for Diploma Programs
C- Minimum for Certificate Programs
  • Must have at least C+ in KCSE
  • Specific subject requirements vary by course
  • Mean grade of C+ and above
  • Must meet cluster points for chosen course

Private Universities

C+ Minimum for Degree Programs
C Minimum for Diploma Programs
D+ Minimum for Certificate Programs
  • Some private universities accept C plain
  • More flexible on subject combinations
  • May have bridging programs for lower grades
  • Direct entry through institutional applications

üéì College Admission Requirements

Medical Training Colleges

C Diploma in Nursing/Clinical Medicine
C- Diploma in Pharmacy/Lab
D+ Certificate Programs
  • Biology and Chemistry required
  • English and Mathematics minimum D+
  • Science subjects must meet specific grades
  • Physical fitness may be required

Teacher Training Colleges

C Primary Teacher Education (PTE)
C- Early Childhood Education (ECDE)
D+ Certificate in ECDE
  • English and Mathematics minimum C plain
  • Two teaching subjects for secondary option
  • Good communication skills
  • Interest in teaching profession

Technical Training Institutes

C- Engineering Diplomas
D+ Technical Certificates
D Artisan Courses
  • Mathematics and Physics/Science required
  • Practical skills assessment
  • Technical drawing may be required
  • Physical fitness for some engineering courses

Business Colleges

C- Business Diplomas
D+ Business Certificates
D Short Courses
  • Mathematics and English minimum D+
  • Commerce/Business Studies preferred
  • Good communication skills
  • Basic computer skills advantage

üí° Important Notes

Cluster Points System

Universities use cluster points calculated from your best performed subjects relevant to your chosen course.

Alternative Entry

Students with lower grades can start with certificate/diploma programs and upgrade to degrees.

Mature Entry

Students over 25 years can apply through mature entry schemes with relevant work experience.

Bridging Programs

Some institutions offer bridging programs for students who don't meet direct entry requirements.
