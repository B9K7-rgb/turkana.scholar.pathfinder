üìù How to Apply for Scholarships

1

Research Opportunities

Identify scholarships that match your qualifications, background, and field of study. Check eligibility criteria carefully.

2

Prepare Documents

Gather required documents: academic transcripts, ID/birth certificate, recommendation letters, personal statement.

3

Write Strong Essays

Create compelling personal statements that highlight your achievements, challenges, and educational goals.

4

Submit Applications

Complete all application forms accurately and submit before deadlines. Keep copies of all submissions.

5

Follow Up

Track your applications and respond promptly to any requests for additional information or interviews.

üìÑ Required Application Documents

Academic Documents

  • KCSE Certificate/Result Slip
  • KCPE Certificate (if required)
  • School leaving certificate
  • Latest academic transcripts
  • Acceptance/admission letter

Personal Documents

  • National ID card or birth certificate
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Parents/guardian ID copies
  • Death certificate (if orphaned)
  • Disability certificate (if applicable)

Supporting Documents

  • Recommendation letters (2-3)
  • Personal statement/essay
  • Proof of income/pay slips
  • Bank statements
  • Ward administrator's letter

üí° Scholarship Application Tips

Start Early

Begin researching and preparing documents months before deadlines. Last-minute applications often miss important details.

Tailor Your Applications

Customize each application to match the specific scholarship criteria. Generic applications are easily overlooked.

Highlight Your Story

Share your unique background, challenges overcome, and how education will help you contribute to Turkana County.

Get Strong Recommendations

Choose referees who know you well and can speak specifically about your abilities and character.

Proofread Carefully

Check for spelling and grammar errors. Ask teachers or mentors to review your applications before submission.

Apply to Multiple Scholarships

Increase your chances by applying to several scholarships. Don't put all your hopes on one application.

‚ö† Important Reminders

Beware of Scams

Never pay money to apply for scholarships. Legitimate scholarships don't require application fees.

Meet Deadlines

Late applications are rarely considered. Set reminders for important deadlines.

Follow Instructions

Read application guidelines carefully and provide exactly what is requested.

Keep Records

Save copies of all applications and supporting documents for reference.
